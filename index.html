Uma's Farmers Market Birthday

Sunday, August 30th · 11am–3pm
The Farm, Green Isle, MN

Address will be sent upon RSVP

The gathering

Come celebrate at the farm for Uma's 29th — an open afternoon of connection, trading, eating, and being outside together. Good food, good people, wide open land.

Make something to trade — optional

Bring something to trade at the market! This is optional, but if you feel like sparking your inner creativity, let loose — and if you don't think of yourself as creative, we believe in you. If you'd like to participate, bring 8–10 of whatever you make, individually packaged at sample size. For example: if you make cookies, package one cookie per bag, times eight. If you make jam, tiny little jars work perfectly. Some ideas:

  • Baked goods or preserves
  • Handmade soap or candles
  • Art, prints, or pressed flowers
  • Knitted or sewn things
  • Dried herbs or garden goods
  • Anything made with care

We'll Eat

Burgers Hot dogs Corn Snacks

The afternoon

Yard games, open fields, and good music. Dress for the outdoors. Bring something to sit on if you like. Come as you are.
