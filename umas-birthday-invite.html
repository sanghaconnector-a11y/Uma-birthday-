Uma's Farmers Market Birthday

August 30th — The Farm, Green Isle, MN

The gathering

Come to the farm for Uma's 29th — an open afternoon of making, trading, eating, and being outside together. Good food, good people, wide open land.

Make something to trade

Bring something handmade to share and swap with what others have made. Anything goes — simple and heartfelt is the spirit.

  • Baked goods or preserves
  • Handmade soap or candles
  • Art, prints, or pressed flowers
  • Knitted or sewn things
  • Dried herbs or garden goods
  • Anything made with care

We'll Eat

Burgers Corn Fruit Charcuterie Mocktails

The afternoon

Yard games, open fields, and good music. Dress for the outdoors. Bring something to sit on if you like. Come as you are.
